About Me

Hi! My name is Lindsay Taylor. I live in South Bend, Indiana and currently work at St. Joe County Public Library. I have two perfect, cuddly, weird cats, a 98-year-old house, and an awesome spouse who cooks amazing dinners for me while I work full time and take classes (he’s the best). I enjoy taking part in local South Bend events, reading about art and art history, antique-shopping, and as of recently, strength training! I graduated from IU South Bend in 2017 with a BFA in graphic design and printmaking, so the transition to library work has been unexpected but so fun; every day is filled with new experiences and challenges.

I’m pursuing my MLIS through IUPUI’s online program. Currently, I work in the Research & Digital Creation department of my local public library which means I get to work with patrons each day on a variety of queries, from tracking down an obituary on microfilm, to locating book titles in our collection and making library cards, all the way to slicing .stl files to create 3D prints in our digital lab!
